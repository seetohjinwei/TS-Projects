Minesweeper

by Jin Wei




Bombs: 0/0

  • Press left click to reveal cell (don't reveal bombs!)
  • Press right click to flag cell as a bomb
  • Flag all bombs to win!
  • Press Flag Button or "F" on keyboard to toggle FlagMode
  • FlagMode reverses left and right clicks!

  • Small - 8 * 8, 10 bombs
  • Medium - 16 * 16, 40 bombs
  • Large - 30 * 16, 99 bombs

  • Press Cheat Button or "C" on keyboard to toggle CheatMode
  • Cheat mode lets you press on a revealed cell to expose surrounding cells
  • *provided that you have flagged the correct number of bombs
  • Basically, makes playing a lot easier! :D
